EL MESI

Lionel Messi, full name Lionel Andrés Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, Messi has won numerous awards, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or titles. He is known for his incredible dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability. Messi spent the majority of his career at FC Barcelona before moving to PSG in 2021. He has also led Argentina to victory in the 2021 Copa América and the 2022 FIFA World Cup.
